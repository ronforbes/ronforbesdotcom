# Welcome to the New RonForbes.com

I'm excited to share the newly redesigned version of my personal website, built from the ground up using modern web technologies.

## What's New

This site is now powered by:

- **Next.js 15** for blazing-fast performance
- **Tailwind CSS** for beautiful, responsive design
- **MDX** for rich, interactive content
- **Shadcn UI** for polished components

## Why the Migration?

I wanted more control over the design and functionality of my site, while also improving the reading experience for you.
